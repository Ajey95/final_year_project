# Pronunciation Videos

This folder contains demonstration videos showing proper mouth movements and pronunciation for each lesson.

## Required Videos

Place pronunciation demonstration videos here with the following naming convention:

### Letter Pronunciation Videos
- `pronunciation_a.mp4` - Shows how to pronounce "அ" (A)
- `pronunciation_aa.mp4` - Shows how to pronounce "ஆ" (AA)
- `pronunciation_la.mp4` - Shows how to pronounce "ல" (LA)
- `pronunciation_ta.mp4` - Shows how to pronounce "த" (TA)

### Word Pronunciation Videos
- `pronunciation_amma.mp4` - Shows how to pronounce "அம்மா" (AMMA)
- `pronunciation_appa.mp4` - Shows how to pronounce "அப்பா" (APPA)

## Video Specifications

- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec recommended)
- **Resolution**: 720p or 1080p
- **Duration**: 3-5 seconds per video
- **Content**: Close-up of mouth/face demonstrating the pronunciation
- **Audio**: Clear pronunciation of the phoneme/word

## Creating Videos

If you don't have professional videos, you can:

1. Record yourself or a therapist pronouncing each sound clearly
2. Focus on the mouth movements
3. Use good lighting
4. Keep videos short (3-5 seconds)
5. Ensure audio is clear and matches the visual

## Fallback

If a video is not found, the system will use:
- Static mouth animation GIFs
- Audio-only pronunciation guides
