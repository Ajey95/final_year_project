# Audio Files

## Letter Pronunciations
Place in `letters/` folder:
- a.mp3 - Pronunciation of அ
- aa.mp3 - Pronunciation of ஆ
- la.mp3 - Pronunciation of ல
- ta.mp3 - Pronunciation of த

## Word Pronunciations
Place in `words/` folder:
- amma.mp3 - Pronunciation of அம்மா (mother)
- appa.mp3 - Pronunciation of அப்பா (father)

## Sound Effects
Place in root `sounds/` folder:
- applause.mp3 - Success sound
- star_collect.mp3- Star collection sound
- whoosh.mp3 - Transition sound
- ding.mp3 - Notification sound

Audio specifications:
- Format: MP3
- Sample rate: 44.1 kHz
- Bitrate: 192 kbps
- Duration: 1-3 seconds (sounds), 1-2 seconds (letters/words)
- Clear native Tamil speaker pronunciation
- Normalized volume levels
